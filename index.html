About Me

While I'm a proficient full-stack developer, my expertise is in building scalable backend (API) services.

I love learning new things. Although learning different programming languages and web development frameworks at University has been helpful, I'm most attracted to applying what I have learnt in solving real customer problems with a business justification. I am currently looking for a junior full-stack developer role in Australia where I can take on collaborative team leadership responsibilites, and gain experience with web development and project management.
